## Chia Coconut Water basic

Add 8 tablepoons of chia seeds to 250ml coconut water, mix well

![image of chia-coconut-2](https://bigbear.org/img/chia-coconut/chia-coconut-2.jpeg)
![image of chia-coconut-3](https://bigbear.org/img/chia-coconut/chia-coconut-3.jpeg)

Store in fridge overnight
